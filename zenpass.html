ZenPass: Your Master Key to Infinite Passwords

Generate unique, strong passwords from one Master Passphrase for all your accounts. No storage, no fees, ultimate privacy!

Step-by-Step Usage:

  • **Enter Master Passphrase:** Your secret key, known only to you.
  • **Enter Service Name:** A unique identifier for the service (e.g., `Google`, `Netflix`). Remember to spell it *exactly* the same way each time, including capitalization and spacing!
  • **Click "Generate Password":** Get your unique, strong password instantly.
  • **Copy & Use:** Copy the generated password and use it with your service.
  • **Close Browser:** When you close the browser, all sensitive data vanishes from memory.
  • **Retrieve Anytime:** To get the password again, just re-enter your Master Passphrase and the exact Service Name.

ZenPass is a generator, not a storage solution. You can either remember one Master Passphrase to generate them all, or save these unique passwords in your favorite password manager.

Important Note: ZenPass does not store any passwords. Your passwords are regenerated each time you provide your Master Passphrase and the exact Service Name. Therefore, we highly recommend using a different, easily recoverable password for your recovery email account, in case you ever forget your Master Passphrase or a specific Service Name.
